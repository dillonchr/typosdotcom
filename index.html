About TYPOS

TYPOS is a homegrown passion project putting together all the best minds (ours, duh) to drum up world class stories and articles. We started TYPOS because we had never attempted anything like it and we were fascinated with books of all kinds. It seemed like a fun thing to try and we were just full of ideas. So we’ve learned a lot and tried out a lot. But probably the most fun and exciting part has been creating content to share with everyone. That’s been a fulfillment all its own.

Submit your stories or artwork

Calling all creators!

We welcome all authors and artists seeking publication to submit content. We are always looking for passionate creators to share their best work. You can submit by filling out this form or just email us at editors@typosmag.com for more info.

We aren’t stingy with spots either. If you have a big story that needs a few pages that’d be awesome. If you want to make a drawing for a cover, WE WOULD ♥ THAT!

Artwork Content Guidelines

TYPOS is currently only printed in black-and-white on a home laserjet. So we really want simple line drawings and things that really pop in monochrome.

Text Content Guidelines

When it comes to stories or articles, we prefer to receive plain text versions. Ideally content would be submitted through the link above and pasted in the form. Receiving plain text lets us type it out on our typewriter and get it scanned in for that authentic typescript feel. And this way we still have the ability to flow the layout as needed or based on a whim.

In terms of the content itself, the only rule we have is that whatever you write has to be passionate. We want to really believe that you feel the text you send us is a self-portrait. We crave content that embodies passion and people’s ideas. Don’t worry about style or grammar. Just let it all out!
